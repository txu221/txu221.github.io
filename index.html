Services

Good things are about to happen

Product recommendation

If a customer wants to open his own barbershop after experiencing our services, we will be happy to provide purchase channels for the products we use to help them start their own business.

Movies and music

Our goal is to bring the best service to customers. When customers are getting their hair cut, we provide their favorite movies or music to create a comfortable atmosphere.

Privacy and confidentiality

Sonicuts is committed to providing every customer with a comfortable and private haircut environment. Unlike other barbers who cut hair between the stairs, our hidden rooms protect the privacy of our customers.

About US


Built by students, for students.

  • 2022-2023

    The Beginning

    The idea for Sonicuts was born
    We found it difficult for students to find their ideal hairstylist, which gave me the idea to open a student-centered barbershop.


  • May 2024

    Sonicuts is established

    As a college student, I used my dorm space to open the Sonicuts studio to provide students with comfortable, affordable and trendy hair services.

  • August 2024

    Service Enhancement

    As our customer base has steadily grown, we have added free drinks, optional music playlists and plan to expand into snack services such as chips to further increase customer satisfaction.

  • December 2024

    Brand Growth

    In order to better serve the student community, I plan to promote Sonicuts through independent websites and publicity activities. That's why I created this website, so that more people can enjoy high-quality haircut services, while constantly improving technology and service quality. We always believe that good things are about to happen.
